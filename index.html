FAQ

Common questions

Is everything really free?

Yes. No login, no paywall, no advertising. The hosting cost runs on a free GitHub Pages tier and the data comes from free public APIs (CoinGecko, Stooq, exchangerate.host).

Do you give buy / sell signals or stock tips?

Never. We publish educational essays, math-grounded calculators, and an open-source backtester so you can learn how markets and trading signals actually work. We don't tell you what to buy.

Are you SEBI / SEC / FCA registered?

No. Epicenter Exchange is an educational platform, not a registered investment adviser, broker, or research analyst. Nothing on the site is investment advice. For personalised advice, consult a SEBI-registered RIA (India) or the equivalent regulated adviser in your country.

Can I use the backtester for real trading?

No. The backtester is for learning how a signal is computed, not for live trading. Real markets add slippage, taxes, latency, and emotional pressure. Past results never guarantee future returns.

Where does the crypto data come from?

CoinGecko's free public API. No API key required. Refreshes roughly every 60 seconds. For trading you should always use your exchange's own data feed.
