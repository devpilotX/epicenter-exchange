FAQ

Common questions

Is everything really free?

Yes. No login, no paywall. The site is funded by Google AdSense — you'll see a small number of ads, never invasive ones.

Do you give buy / sell signals or stock tips?

Never. We publish educational essays, math-grounded calculators, and an open-source backtester so you can learn how markets and trading signals actually work.

Are you SEBI / SEC / FCA registered?

No. Epicenter Exchange is an educational platform, not a registered investment adviser, broker, or research analyst. Nothing on the site is investment advice.

Can I use the backtester for real trading?

No. The backtester is for learning how a signal is computed, not for live trading. Real markets add slippage, taxes, latency, and emotional pressure. Past results never guarantee future returns.

Where does the data come from?

Server-proxied Yahoo Finance for indices/stocks, CryptoCompare for crypto historical, CoinGecko for crypto spot. Refreshes roughly every 60 seconds.
