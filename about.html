About

A one-person attempt to make finance honest.

Epicenter Exchange is built and maintained by one person. It is a non-profit, free, open-source project — no team, no investors, no paid product.

Why this exists

Most finance content online is paywalled, sponsored, or thinly disguised marketing for someone's brokerage. This site is the opposite — an open library of explanations, calculators, and code that anyone can read, fork, and learn from.

What we will never do

  • ❌ Give buy / sell / hold recommendations on specific securities
  • ❌ Sell courses, signals, or subscriptions
  • ❌ Promise returns or “guaranteed” accuracy
  • ❌ Manage your money or accept deposits
  • ❌ Run paid ads or affiliate referrals

What we will do

  • ✅ Publish educational explainers
  • ✅ Share open-source signal code with transparent backtests
  • ✅ Provide free, no-signup calculators
  • ✅ Answer general questions via the contact page (best-effort, no SLA)
Regulatory status: The author is NOT a SEBI Registered Investment Adviser, SEC RIA, FCA-authorised adviser, or any other licensed financial professional. Nothing on this site is personalised advice.
