Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

1. Who we are

Epicenter Exchange (“we”, “us”) is a non-profit educational website operated by an individual based in India. We are not a SEBI / SEC / FCA registered entity. Contact: see contact page.

2. What we collect

  • Contact form data — name, email, and message you submit. Processed by Formspree and emailed to us.
  • Newsletter (optional) — email address, processed by Buttondown.
  • Server logs — GitHub Pages hosts this site and logs IP addresses for abuse prevention. We have no access to these logs.
  • No analytics, no advertising, no third-party trackers, no cookies set by us.

3. Why we process it

Solely to (a) reply to your contact-form messages and (b) send you the newsletter if you subscribed. We never sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers.

4. Legal basis

Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) and legitimate interest in responding to enquiries (Art. 6(1)(f)). Under India’s DPDP Act 2023, your consent is the basis for all processing.

5. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data at any time. Email us via the contact page. We respond within 30 days.

6. Data retention

Contact messages: kept for 24 months then deleted. Newsletter subscribers: kept until you unsubscribe (every email has a one-click unsubscribe link).

7. Children

This site is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect their data.

8. International transfers

Formspree (US) and Buttondown (US) process data under their respective standard contractual clauses. GitHub Pages serves the site globally via CDN.

9. Changes

If this policy changes materially we will note it on the home page for 30 days.
