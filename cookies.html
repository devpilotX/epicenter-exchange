Cookie Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

1. The short version

We do not set any tracking or advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any cross-site tracker.

2. What is technically set

  • localStorage — our calculators may save your last inputs in your browser so you don’t have to re-enter them. This stays on your device and is never sent to a server. You can clear it anytime via your browser settings.
  • Third-party services you opt into: if you submit the contact form, Formspree may set its own cookies on its domain. If you subscribe to the newsletter, Buttondown may set its own cookies on its domain. See their respective policies.

3. No consent banner?

Correct. Because we set zero cookies of our own and use no third-party analytics, the EU ePrivacy Directive does not require a banner for us. We chose this deliberately to keep your visit private.

4. Do Not Track

There is nothing to track. Your DNT header is respected by default.
