Equity markets at a glance

Reference notes — not real-time quotes. For live equity data, use your broker.

🇮🇳 India

NIFTY 50 & Sensex

~14% historical CAGR over 25 years on a total-return basis, with multiple 30–60% drawdowns along the way. STT, LTCG (12.5% above ₹1.25 lakh), and expense ratios remove ~1–1.5% per year.

Reference tickers: ^NSEI · RELIANCE.NS · TCS.NS · HDFCBANK.NS · INFY.NS

🇺🇸 United States

S&P 500 & NASDAQ

~10% historical CAGR including dividends. NASDAQ skews tech and is more volatile. Currency risk matters for Indian investors holding US assets via LRS.

Reference tickers: ^GSPC · ^IXIC · AAPL · MSFT · GOOGL · NVDA

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

FTSE 100 & FTSE 250

FTSE 100 is dividend-heavy (banks, oil, miners, pharma), FTSE 250 more domestically-tilted. Long-term real returns have lagged US, but valuations are typically more reasonable.

Reference tickers: ^FTSE · ^FTMC · HSBA.L · BP.L · VOD.L · AZN.L

🪙 Crypto

Bitcoin, Ethereum & majors

60–100% historical annualised volatility. Every cycle has had a 70%+ drawdown. In India: 30% flat tax, 1% TDS, no loss offset. Custody risk is real.
