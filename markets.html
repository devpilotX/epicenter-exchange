Equity market context

Reference notes. For live equity data, see the dashboard above.

🇮🇳 India

NIFTY 50 & Sensex

~14% historical CAGR over 25 years on a total-return basis. Multiple 30-60% drawdowns. STT, LTCG (12.5% above ₹1.25 lakh), expense ratios remove ~1-1.5% per year.

Backtest tickers: ^nse · reliance.in · tcs.in · hdfcbank.in · infy.in

🇺🇸 United States

S&P 500 & NASDAQ

~10% historical CAGR including dividends. NASDAQ skews tech and is more volatile. Currency risk matters for Indian investors via LRS.

Backtest tickers: ^spx · ^ndx · aapl.us · msft.us · nvda.us

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

FTSE 100 & FTSE 250

FTSE 100 dividend-heavy (banks, oil, miners, pharma); FTSE 250 more domestic. Long-term real returns lag US but valuations typically lower.

Backtest tickers: ^ftm · hsba.uk · bp.uk · azn.uk

🪙 Crypto

Bitcoin, Ethereum & majors

60-100% historical annualised volatility. Every cycle has had a 70%+ drawdown. India: 30% flat tax, 1% TDS, no loss offset. Custody risk is real.
